MISSION
I am interested in understanding human behavior. Honest exploration is very important to me and a necessary constant in my life. I am always searching for growth in myself, which I find can often come form a state of discomfort. I enjoy dissection human behavior, observing chaos to find patterns, and learning how the brain works.


WORK
I currently work at HelloFresh as a product designer in Berlin, Germany on the Supply Chain Management team where I collaborate with a team of product owners, and developers to create innovative design solutions and improve our user's overall experiences.


SANS DESIGN
I recently got back from a year of traveling and working around the world, with a little help from Remote Year. When not working, I spend my time exploring creative endeavors (most currently documenting sounds and conversations over the past year of traveling), shooting film photography, and having the occasional life crisis wondering what I am doing with my life.
